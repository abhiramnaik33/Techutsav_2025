Mindscape

April 25-26, 2025

Join the ultimate quiz competition that will test your knowledge, skills, and teamwork! Mindscape is a 2-phase event, open to all students, where teams of 3-4 members will compete to prove their expertise.

  • Eligibility: Open to students from all years.
  • Team Size: 3 to 4 members.
  • Phase 1: Written Quiz (MCQs) - Test your knowledge with multiple-choice questions.
  • Phase 2: Final Round (Oral Quiz) - Top teams compete in a fast-paced quiz across various domains.
  • Exciting prizes for the winners!
  • Venue: Phase 1 - Classroom 3 | Phase 2 - Halamma Hall.
